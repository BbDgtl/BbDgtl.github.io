About

I bridge the gap between design and development.

I am a multi-faceted design enthusiast & problem solver with a background in retail management and frontend web development. I'm currently finishing my studies with NumericALL where I'm upgrading my skills in full-stack web development. I'm interested in the visual aspect of the web, its usability, accessibility and I'm curious about the user experience.

I'm also interested in community-minded projects, such as sustainability and other ecological issues currently affecting our planet, to both human and animal rights. I'm particularly passionate about dogs. We don't deserve dogs, and I believe we owe it to them to give voice to those who can't speak for themselves. In my spare time, I enjoy watching YouTube tutorials, playing with my dog Rocky, cooking and also playing video games. I also enjoy travelling when I have the time.

I am currently looking for a position that would allow me to create functional and visually appealing user experiences on the web. I would like to use my experience as both a web developer and a ux/ui designer to create products that are competitive in the market as well as provide a benefit to my organization. I'm open to all opportunities that will allow me to continue to grow as a "full-stack designer".
